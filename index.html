Timing dashboard — when highs & lows tend to happen, with Gann, Hurst, astro & Fibonacci methods backtested per stock. To refresh data: double-click refresh.bat. To change the stock list: edit tickers.txt (one symbol per line), then refresh.
Read this first. Exact-date-and-time prediction of stock highs/lows is not reliably possible — no method does it consistently. This dashboard takes every famous timing school (W.D. Gann's counts, Hurst cycles, lunar/astro, Fibonacci) plus plain statistics, tests each against this stock's real history, and shows the honest hit rate next to every claim. The forecast is where methods agree. Treat dates as probability windows, not appointments. Earnings, Fed days and headline news override everything.
